Good people of the Mechanical MOOC,

For your delight and optimum experience, we offer you the opportunity to learn with a small group over the course of the Mechanical MOOC. With your MOOCster group, you can:

  • Meet other smarties looking to learn the same things as you
  • Ask each other questions and enjoy a smaller network of support
  • Increase the likelihood that you’ll actually finish the darn course

We’ll even play matchmaker on your behalf, grouping you with peers intended to maximize your learning experience.

We won’t put you in a group until you let us know you’re into the idea. After all, some folks might want to learn solo. But if you want to get as hip as online learning gets, let P2PU fix you up in team. We’ll let you know when it’s time to get crackin'.

Why do I have to confirm? Because your team needs you!

Those who sign-up will be placed in small groups, work on individual projects, review and discuss each other's work and hopefully participate for the duration of the course. The other members of your group rely on you to add your voice to the conversations and give feedback.

We've had more than 10,000 people register and while that is super exciting it also means that the course facilitators, including Mitch and Natalie, won't be able to participate much in the online groups. We are here to create the basic structure and support the community as much as we can, but we rely on you to bring the course to life.

If you think it'll be hard to keep up, you can always defer until the next round. And even if you don't sign up, you'll be able to watch all seminar videos and even participate in the main G+ community.
